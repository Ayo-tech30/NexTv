Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

YOUR PRIVACY MATTERS

1. Information We Collect

NexTV operates entirely in your browser. We collect only what's necessary to provide you with a great streaming experience:

  • Account info: Name and email address you provide when registering (stored locally in your browser)
  • Watch history: Titles you've watched, stored locally on your device
  • Preferences: Theme settings, watchlist, and download records — all stored in your browser's local storage

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external server.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data stays on your device and is used solely to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Remember your watchlist and watch history
  • Save your theme and display preferences
  • Track what you've downloaded for easy access

3. Third-Party Services

NexTV uses third-party services to deliver content:

  • Video streams: Served by vidsrc, 2embed, and similar providers. Their own privacy policies apply.
  • Poster & backdrop images: Loaded from TMDB (The Movie Database). See TMDB's Privacy Policy.
  • Fonts: Google Fonts — see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

NexTV uses your browser's localStorage (not cookies) to store your account data, preferences, and history. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data or using the clear history option in your profile.

5. Children's Privacy

NexTV is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of NexTV after changes means you accept the revised policy.
