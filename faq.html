Downloads & Offline

Open any movie or show and click the Download button. It saves to your Downloads page (accessible from the nav). Once saved, you can watch it anytime — even without internet.

Note: Downloads are stored in your browser's local storage, so they stay on that device/browser only.

Downloaded titles are cached using your browser's built-in storage — each title takes very little space (just the metadata and poster). The service worker caches all 366+ posters automatically on first visit so they always load instantly.

Go to Downloads in the navigation, find the title, and click the trash icon. You can also clear all downloads at once with the "Clear All" button.

Account & Profile

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email. If password reset isn't available yet, you can create a new account with the same email. User data (watchlist, history) is stored locally on your device.

Go to your Profile page and scroll to the bottom. Click "Delete Account". This removes all your data permanently including watchlist, history, reviews, and playlists. This action cannot be undone.

Click the My List button on any movie or show card or detail page. It saves to your personal Watchlist, accessible from the nav. Sign in to keep your list synced across sessions.
